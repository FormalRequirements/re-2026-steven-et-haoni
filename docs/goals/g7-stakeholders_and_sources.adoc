[[G7]]
=== G.7 Stakeholders and requirements sources

NOTE: Groups of people who can affect the project or be affected by it, and other places to consider for information about the project and system (see {Handbook}).

WARNING: This chapter should not be empty (following the _Minimum Requirements Outcome Principle_, p.27 of the {handbook}).

The requirements originate from occupants, information system security managers, energy managers, and institutional decision-makers, in accordance with existing security and energy policies.

The requirements originate from:

- occupants, particularly people with disabilities,

- cybersecurity and physical security experts,

- healthcare and assistance professionals,

- energy and environmental policy stakeholders.
