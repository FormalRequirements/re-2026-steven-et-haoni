[[G6]]
=== G.6 Limitations and Exclusions

NOTE: Aspects that the system need not address (see {Handbook}).

The following limitations and exclusions define the boundaries of the Smart Home
System (SHS). They are intended to clarify the scope of the system and manage
stakeholder expectations.


//---- Limitation / Exclusion
[[g6-no-external-infra]]
^`G.6.{counter:g6}`^  
The Smart Home System does not manage external energy infrastructures or public
utility networks beyond local monitoring and optimization.
//----


//---- Limitation / Exclusion
[[g6-no-human-replacement]]
^`G.6.{counter:g6}`^  
The Smart Home System does not replace human intervention services such as law
enforcement, firefighters, or medical personnel.
//----


//---- Limitation / Exclusion
[[g6-no-absolute-protection]]
^`G.6.{counter:g6}`^  
The Smart Home System does not guarantee absolute protection against all physical
or cyber threats; its role is limited to prevention, detection, and assistance.
//----


//---- Limitation / Exclusion
[[g6-no-physical-intervention]]
^`G.6.{counter:g6}`^  
The Smart Home System does not physically intervene during emergency situations;
it only detects events and triggers alerts or guidance mechanisms.
//----


//---- Limitation / Exclusion
[[g6-device-dependency]]
^`G.6.{counter:g6}`^  
The correct operation of the Smart Home System depends on the availability and
proper functioning of physical devices such as sensors, detectors, and network
infrastructure.
//----
