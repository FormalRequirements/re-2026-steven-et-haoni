The implementation of the Smart Home System is expected to provide significant
improvements in terms of security, occupant assistance, and energy efficiency.
These benefits directly address the limitations identified in the current situation
(see G.2).

By integrating physical security, cybersecurity, and occupant safety mechanisms
within a single intelligent system, the Smart Home enhances both protection and
quality of life for its occupants.

[[g3-benefit-security]]

Improved protection against physical intrusions and unauthorized digital access,
resulting in a higher overall level of dwelling security.
