[[E2]]
=== E.2 Components

NOTE: List of elements of the environment that may affect or be affected by the
system and project. This includes actors and physical components external to the
Smart Home System (SHS) with which it may interact (see {Handbook}).

The environment of the Smart Home System includes human actors and physical
elements that are not part of the SHS itself but influence its behavior or are
affected by its operation. Interactions with these elements occur indirectly,
through sensors, actuators, or information exchanges.


//// Human actors

//---- Component
[[e2-occupants]]
^`E.2.{counter:e2}`^  
**Occupants** :: Primary users of the Smart Home. Their interactions, needs, and
constraints are characterized by their `User profile` (E.1).
////


//---- Component
[[e2-emergency-services]]
^`E.2.{counter:e2}`^  
**Emergency Services** :: External organizations including police, firefighters,
and medical services (SAMU) that may be notified or granted access in critical
situations.
////


//---- Component
[[e2-intruders]]
^`E.2.{counter:e2}`^  
**Intruders** :: Non-authorized persons whose presence or actions must be detected
and countered by the system to ensure the security of the dwelling.
////
