[[E1]]
=== E.1 Glossary

NOTE: Clear and precise definitions of all the vocabulary specific to the application
domain, including technical terms, words from ordinary language used in a special
meaning, and acronyms (see {Handbook}).

WARNING: This chapter should not be empty (following the Glossary Principle, p.27 of
the {handbook}).


//---- Term
[[e1-shs]]
^`E.1.{counter:e1}`^  
**SHS (Smart Home System)** :: Software and hardware system responsible for managing,
monitoring, and assisting the smart domestic environment, including security,
safety, and assistance functions.
//----


//---- Term
[[e1-mi]]
^`E.1.{counter:e1}`^  
**MI (Smart Home)** :: Domestic environment integrating intelligent systems that
interact with physical elements of the dwelling.
//----


//---- Term
[[e1-bed]]
^`E.1.{counter:e1}`^  
**Bed** :: Physical element of the domestic environment, not part of the SHS, but
potentially interacting indirectly with it through sensors or actuators.
//----


//---- Term
[[e1-adaptable-furniture]]
^`E.1.{counter:e1}`^  
**Adaptable furniture** :: Furniture capable of changing its configuration to meet
the specific physical or ergonomic needs of the user.
//----


//---- Term
[[e1-adaptable-bed]]
^`E.1.{counter:e1}`^  
**Adaptable bed** :: Furniture element capable of adjusting automatically or manually
to the user’s needs, particularly in the context of reduced mobility or disability.
//----


//---- Term
[[e1-user-profile]]
^`E.1.{counter:e1}`^  
**User profile** :: Set of characteristics describing a user’s physical, sensory,
and cognitive abilities, preferences, and constraints, used by the SHS to adapt its
behavior.
//----


//---- Term
[[e1-standards]]
^`E.1.{counter:e1}`^  
**Local standards** :: Set of applicable legal, technical, and regulatory rules
defining constraints related to safety, security, data protection, and building
compliance.
//----
